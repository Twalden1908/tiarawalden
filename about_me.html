About Me

My name is Tiara Walden, and I am a Full-stack Developer from South Jersey. I have a Bachelor of Science in Management Information Systems with a minor in Marketing. I currently work in logics where I come up with may new ways to solve problems. I am both driven and self-motivated, and I am continually experimenting with new technologies and techniques. I am very passionate about technology and strive to better myself as a developer. My Motto is, "How can I make this better?"

When I am not working, you can find me in my local library, watching 90 Day Fiance, being a wonderful mother to my orange tabby, baking cupcakes, or listening to my favorite podcast; The Read.

My current personal project is an interactive workbook for financial, physical, and mental health.
